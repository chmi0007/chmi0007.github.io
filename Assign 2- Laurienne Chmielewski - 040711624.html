Shrooms

Chicken of the Woods

Chicken of the woods is an edible mushroon that has a similar taste as chicken. Found on dead and drying hardwood trees such as hemlock, oak, cherry or beach. It is not recommended to consume chicken of the woods if found on a live tree or any tree other then previously mentioned

Chanterelle

Chanterelles and one of the most delisius mushrooms to be found. Only found in the wild as cultivation of this mushroom has not been successful for many mushroom growers. Best found on the growed in pine forestes. Has a nutty taste.

Boulet

Boulet is a highly sought after mushroom by many mushroom enthusiast. This mushroom is easy to find and is very plentaful in a mixed wood and coniferous forest. The cap is a porus texture and may bruse easily. If the bruse turns blue you must know how to prepare this mushroom correctly or Do not consume it.

Medicinal Mushroom

Turkey Tail

Turkey Tail is a medicinal mushroom. Best found on decaying hardwood trees. Use as a Cancer preventative in a herbal tea. This mushroom is found throughout the world and the Chinese medical industry actually prescribe this mushroom in a medicinal form to fight against breast cancer

Chaga

Chaga is an medicinal mushroom that has been used by indigenous people worldwide. Best found on Living Silver Birth or Elm trees. Not recommended to harvest of dying or dead trees. Use as a Cancer preventative in a herbal tea. Warning! this mushroom may become toxic to the liver if you over consume it

Reishi

Reishi is an medicinal mushroom which resembles the shape of a kidney. Best found on dead hardwood trees. Use as a Cancer perventative in a herbal tea.The reishi mushroom is widley used in the Chinese medical industry for a number of different purposes

When out hiking and foraging of edibles or herbal medicines be sure to do your research 1, 2, 3 times before you even think of trying to produce your own concoction
