Ferris Bueller's Day Off

This is a movie that all students within the educational system can enjoy.
Ferris Bueller is a young high school student who uses a fictional illness to take a day off of school. First he rescues his girlfriend Sloane from a redundant day of classes to attend a fictional funeral with her cloak-and-dagger father. Together they pick up their chronically depressed friend and take him out and to enjoy everything that life has to offer 3 vivacious teenagers.
This is a movie that will inspire all teens to Take Life by the Horns and Enjoy Oneself.
